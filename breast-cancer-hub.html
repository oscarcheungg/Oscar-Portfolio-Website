Breast Cancer Hub

An app designed to help with early detection and prevention of breast cancer.

Interactive Figma prototype on the left. Still in working progress!

Project Overview

For this project, I led the front-end development team for a more intuitive and user-friendly self-check breast cancer app. We had to collaborate closely with the UI/UX and backend teams to ensure timely delivery of each screen for the app. Our main goal for this app was to create this early detection tool for breast cancer and ensuring that the UI of the app was suitable for all age groups.

Main Objective

How can we empower users to perform self-checks for early signs of breast cancer through an app?
