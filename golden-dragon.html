A website designed to enhance visibility and streamline operations for my parent's local Chinese restaurant, Golden Dragon.

Project Overview

For this project, I built the Figma prototype and worked with two other members in bringing my website idea to life. We used my Hi-Fi wireframes and other elements within my UI design as inspiration to make a website that closely resembles my idea. Our objective was to enhance the restaurant's online presence, as my parents have never had a website besides Facebook, by creating an intuitive and seamless user experience that enables easy online ordering and highlights the restaurant's offering. We were able to successfully develop a digital platform that reflects the quality and authenticity of the Golden Dragon dining experience while boosting customer engagement and driving growth for our family-run business. :)

Main Objective

How can we enhance the online presence of Golden Dragon by creating a user-friendly website that improves customer engagement and streamlines the online ordering experience?
