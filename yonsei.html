Image Recoginition Model

A deep learning model designed to classify interior design styles by leveraging image recognition techniques, developed as part of an AI and Design program at Yonsei University!

Project Overview

For this project, I built an image recognition model designed to classify over various interior design styles, leveraging both TensorFlow and Google Colab. Collaborating with peers during my study abroad at Yonsei International Summer School, I applied AI techniques to enhance design classification accuracy and streamline the analysis of living spaces. This project taught me more about machine learning with design optimization, offering a sophisticated tool for identifying and categorizing diverse design elements. The final model reflects the intersection of AI and creative design, contributing to my knowledge in predictive analysis.

Main Objective

How can I develop an AI-powered image recognition model to accurately classify interior design styles, enhancing design workflows and advancing the integration of machine learning in creative industries?
